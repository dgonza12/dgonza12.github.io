Battle Guns

Spring 2016

Description:

A sort of clone of Overwatch, made using LUA on the Roblox engine. I was able to make 3 modes: Defense(where the one team tries to capture a point). King of the hill (where both teams fight for control of a point). Escort the payload(where one team escorts the payload to a destination)Link to the game about Roblox

Responsibilities:

The Roblox engine handles creating servers, users, respawning. What I had to do was make the everything else which included making the scripts for weapons, game modes, UI, and the game script that ran everything.

About Overwatch:

Overwatch is a game by Blizzard, a team based first person shooter. The game has a lot of characters, they all have unique abilities. This idea intrigued me, and I wanted to make my own characters and maps. So I decided to make a little clone of it to show case my own characters, it is not for profit at all just for fun. about Overwatch
