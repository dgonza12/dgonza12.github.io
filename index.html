About

Hey, I'm Danny. I'm from the Bay Area; started in the East Bay, then studied in San Francisco, and now I'm currently in Palo Alto.

My current hobbies are learning to play the piano, painting using water color, and taking pictures using a Polaroid camera.

Middle College High School
From 2011 to 2015 I attended Middle College High School at Contra Costa College. MCHS is an early college highschool, which meant that while earning my high school diploma I also earned an associates degree in math and science.
My first year I was in the Robotic's Club. Which is where I started learning about programming, we used C++ for our Robot. Also I helped with that logo; it's supposed to be a Tetris piece because our robot was L shaped. Though, at the end my first year the teacher in charge left and so the club was only that year.
In the meantime I joined another club called MESA (MATH ENGINEERING SCIENCE ACHIEVEMENT). My senior year, I help start a club called the Computer Science Club, where we ran weekly meetings and worked on individual projects, mostly I taught the underclassmen programming basics.

Overall the curriculum was more intense because it included the required high school courses as well as college courses. However this meant that once I went to a 4 year university I would come in with abundant college credit.
